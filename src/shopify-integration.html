What happens when you get an order from Shopify?

Our seamless integration automatically handles your entire order fulfillment process from start to finish. Here's exactly what happens behind the scenes when a customer places an order.

Sync your store

1. Customer places order

A customer visits your Shopify store and places an order for one of your canvas prints.

2. Order is synced to Printumo

The order is automatically synced from Shopify to Printumo with all shipping details and customer information.

3. Order is ready for fulfillment

The order enters our fulfillment queue and is prepared for production at our printing facility.

4. Order is printed

Your customer's canvas print is professionally printed using premium materials and quality-checked before packaging.

5. Order is shipped with tracking

The finished print is shipped and a tracking link is automatically added to the Shopify order, triggering a tracking email to your customer.

6. Customer receives their order

Your customer receives their high-quality canvas print, complete with your branding and packaging. The order is now complete and your customer is happy with their purchase.
