Features

High Quality Canvas

Our canvas prints are crafted with high-quality materials to ensure lasting durability and vibrant colors.

With customizable sizing options and framing choices, each piece is made to meet the highest standards, giving your artwork a premium finish.

Our canvas is 344 gsm in weight and 17.5 mil thick.

Framing

Our floater frames are crafted from poplar hardwood, giving your canvas the appearance of floating within the frame.

The frame is 1 cm (0.4”) wide from the front and 4 cm (1.6”) deep from the side.

The frames comes in black, white and oak to suit any artwork.

Ready to hang

Each print comes ready to hang with pre-installed sawtooth hardware and rubber bumpers to protect your wall and keep your artwork perfectly aligned.
