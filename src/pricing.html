About us

For Artist, by Artists

Our journey began as artists selling our own art through our webshop, but it was difficult to find a reliable print-on-demand partner that offered the variety of sizes and framing options we needed.

Frustrated by the lack of options for artists and creative individuals, we decided to built a platform designed specifically for canvas and posters.

At Printumo, we are dedicated to delivering the quality, flexibility, and support artists need, so your creative vision can shine in every print
