Our premium canvas prints are crafted using museum-quality giclée printing technology, ensuring exceptional detail and color accuracy that brings your artwork to life. Each print is carefully produced on archival-grade canvas that's designed to last for generations.

  • Material: Poly cotton blend with a matte/satin finish. Scratch, crack, & warp resistant. 344 gsm weight & 17.5 mil thickness.
  • Printing: Strong vibrant colors from sustainable water based HP latex inks and protected with UV coatings.
  • Strecher bar: A depth of 3.2cm and made from 100% renewable, kiln-dried, knot-free, finger jointed wood
  • Floater frames: 4.45cm deep solid wood, black, white or oak finish.
  • Whiteness & Brightness: 126 min & 98 min

Please note: Due to the production process of the canvases, please allow for slight size deviations with a tolerance +/- 1/8" (3.2mm).
