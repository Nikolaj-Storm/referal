The Artist's Journey

Amanda Heath is a contemporary artist known for her vibrant, colorful artwork. With a passion for creating unique visual experiences, she began her artistic journey over a decade ago. As her work gained popularity, she needed a reliable way to offer high-quality prints to her growing audience.

Amanda in her art studio working on a new piece

"I always struggled with finding a print-on-demand service that could offer the larger sizes my clients wanted," Amanda explains. "My artwork is often quite detailed and vibrant, so having the option to print in extra large formats was essential to showcase the details properly."

Discovering Printumo

When Amanda discovered Printumo, she was immediately impressed by the range of canvas sizes available, including the extra large formats her clients had been requesting.

"Printumo's platform makes it incredibly easy to sell my art online. Their extra large canvas prints are exactly what my clients have been asking for, and the quality is exceptional."

Business Transformation

Since partnering with Printumo, Amanda has experienced a significant boost in her online sales. Her customers frequently comment on the exceptional quality of the canvas prints, particularly the color accuracy which is crucial for her vibrant style.

"What I love most about Printumo is how they've simplified the entire process. I can focus on creating new artwork while they handle the printing, framing, and shipping. This has freed up so much of my time and allowed me to be more productive as an artist."

Amanda's artwork on display in a customer's home

Today, Amanda continues to grow her online presence and reach new collectors around the world. With Printumo handling the fulfillment side of her business, she can dedicate more time to her creative process and expanding her artistic portfolio.
