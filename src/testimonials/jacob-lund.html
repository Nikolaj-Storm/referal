The Artist's Journey

Jacob Lund, a professional photographer and visual artist, has been creating stunning visual artwork for over 15 years. His journey with Printumo began when he was looking for a reliable print-on-demand service that could handle his growing global audience.

Jacob in his photography studio in Copenhagen

"Before discovering Printumo, I struggled with finding a POD service that could consistently deliver high-quality canvas prints at a variety of sizes," Jacob recalls. "My customers often wanted large-format prints, and other services simply couldn't handle the quality I expected."

The Printumo Difference

After partnering with Printumo, Jacob was able to offer his artwork on premium canvas prints in a wide range of sizes, including extra-large formats that were previously unavailable to him.

"The quality of Printumo's canvas prints exceeded my expectations, and my clients immediately noticed the difference. The colors are vibrant, the canvas texture enhances the depth of my photography, and the frames are professionally finished."

Explosive Business Growth

Within just six months of switching to Printumo, Jacob's monthly revenue increased by over 70%. Today, his canvas print sales generate more than €30,000 monthly.

"What really sets Printumo apart is their global fulfillment network. My customers in Europe, North America, and Australia all receive their orders quickly, with consistent quality regardless of which production facility handles their order."

Jacob's artwork is shipped to collectors in 28 countries

Jacob has expanded his product offerings and now sells his artwork to collectors and art enthusiasts in 28 countries, all while maintaining full creative control over his business.
